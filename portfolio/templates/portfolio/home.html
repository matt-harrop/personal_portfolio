About

I am a technical guy who got a job in the IT department of the bank I was working at while putting off taking the MCAT and applying to medical school. I found that I had a lot more interest in working in computer systems and writing code than my studies and was fortunate enough to impress someone in IT enough that they recommended I take their analyst position when they got promoted.

I have now worked as a systems analyst, an applications engineer, and now even have the uncommon privilege of working on an IBM Power System. I worked as a developer briefly and am finishing up my master's in software engineering, and looking forward to working through the list of personal software projects that I've come up with over the last few years.
